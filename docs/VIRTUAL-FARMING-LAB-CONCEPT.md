# Virtual Farming Lab — The Learning Engine of EDEN

> The Virtual Farming Lab is not Layer 3. It is the substrate that all layers stand on.

## The Shift

The original concept described "The Dreamer" as Layer 3 — a simulation that runs during off-peak cycles. That was an illustration of the idea. What it actually is:

**The Virtual Farming Lab is the scientific testing ground for a self-orchestrating farm.** Agents hypothesize. The Lab validates. Validated patterns become flight rules. Reality calibrates. The cycle repeats. Everything in EDEN — rules, expertise, insights, the Codex — traces back to this loop.

## What Each Layer Actually Draws From the Lab

### Flight Rules — AI-Written, Simulation-Validated

The agents write the rules. SENTINEL proposes: "If water_reserve < 80% AND cme_eta > 48h, begin stockpiling." The Lab tests it across 500 simulated scenarios. 98.6% success rate. PROMOTED. The rule isn't a human heuristic — it's an AI hypothesis with statistical evidence.

Without the Lab: the rule is an opinion.
With the Lab: the rule has a p-value.

### Triage — AI-Reasoned, Simulation-Informed

The agents decide triage. VITA says "save spinach for vitamin C." AQUA says "the water math says potato." HESTIA says "crew morale needs the tomato harvest." These are competing objectives that an LLM resolves through deliberation.

The Lab pre-computes: "In 200 simulated water crises, saving potato over spinach yields +340 kcal/sol but crew iron drops below threshold by Sol 280." Now the council debate isn't opinion — it's informed by numbers.

### Council Expertise — AI-Generated, Simulation-Tested

Each agent arrives at a crisis with context from thousands of simulated runs. SENTINEL doesn't just "care about safety" — SENTINEL has seen 200 simulated CMEs and knows that flowering wheat is the critical vulnerability. That knowledge was generated by running the Lab, not by writing a better prompt.

### Novel Insights — AI-Discovered, Simulation-Proven

"Reducing potato DLI by 15% during BBCH 40-45 increases tuber yield 8% on Mars."

No human asked for this. No Syngenta KB document contains it. An agent (ORACLE) asked: "What if I vary light across growth stages?" The Lab ran the sweep. The math showed the insight. The LLM interpreted it: "Mars solar spectrum exceeds potato radiation tolerance during tuber initiation — reducing light prevents photoinhibition."

This is genuine agricultural discovery. The agent had the curiosity. The simulation had the rigor.

## The Simulation Engine (What's Built)

`eden/domain/simulation.py` — 800 lines of pure Python. No external imports.

### Crop Growth Model
- GDD thermal time (Growing Degree Days — standard agronomy since 1960)
- Liebig's Law: growth limited by most deficient factor (multiplicative stress)
- 7 stress factors: temperature, water, DLI light, VPD disease, radiation (BBCH-aware), bolting, tuberization

### Environmental Models
- VPD via Tetens equation (the CEA management metric)
- DLI in mol/m2/day (the real light metric, not lux)
- Root zone temperature (distinct from air — potato/spinach critical)
- Resource chain: solar → power → desal → water → crops (95% closed-loop recovery)

### Crop Parameters
6 crops parameterized from NASA CEA research + Syngenta KB:
- Per-crop transpiration rates by growth stage
- Per-crop DLI targets and photoperiod
- Per-crop VPD targets
- Harvest index from published data (Apogee wheat 0.42 from Utah State)
- Space cultivar data (ISS VEGGIE, EDEN ISS, BIO-Plex programs)

### Monte Carlo Engine
- N runs (50-100) with gaussian perturbation on all parameters
- Environmental variance: temperature, humidity, event severity
- Outputs: yield loss %, confidence intervals (p5/p50/p95), nutritional preservation (kcal/sol, protein/sol), water efficiency (kcal/liter), survival probability
- Deterministic: same seed = same numbers, every time
- Performance: 1,800 evaluations in 57ms

### Strategy Comparison
4 scenarios (CME, water failure, disease, dust storm) × 3 strategies each.
Always includes "Do Nothing" as baseline.
Strategies ranked by outcome distribution, not point estimate.

### Integration
- Agent tool: `run_simulation(scenario_type)` callable by ORACLE
- API endpoint: `POST /api/simulate/{scenario_type}`
- Produces structured JSON for dashboard rendering
