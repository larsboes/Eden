# **AGRICULTURE ON MARS**

**SYNGENTA**

Syngenta is a global AgTech leader dedicated to bringing plant potential to life. Operating in over 100 countries, we combine cutting-edge biology with digital innovation to help farmers produce healthy, affordable food while protecting the planet. From AI-driven crop protection to precision seeding and regenerative agriculture, we leverage massive datasets to solve the world’s most pressing food security challenges. Your mission: Use technology to bridge the gap between complex agricultural data and sustainable, real-world farming solutions. Join us in feeding the world, one line of code at a time.

**Description**

NASA's planned missions to Mars in the late 2030s raise a critical challenge: how to grow nutritionally balanced food for astronauts during long-duration space travel and extended surface stays. Participants are asked to design an autonomous AI agent system that manages a Martian greenhouse and demonstrates its capabilities through a working simulation or digital twin. The solution should optimize nutrient output, dietary balance, and resource efficiency for a crew of four astronauts, while also offering insights that could benefit autonomous agriculture in extreme environments on Earth.

Agriculture's Next Frontier: Feeding Humans on the Red Planet

# **3\. Case Summary**

## **A. What is the current problem?**

NASA is targeting the late 2030s to send astronauts to Mars, a trip that could take up to 9 months each way. Figuring out how to grow nutritionally balanced food on the Red Planet is a critical mission and may also help us grow food in increasingly extreme conditions on Earth.

## **B. What is the expected final product?**

Hackers will design an autonomous AI agent system to manage a Martian greenhouse. The solution should consist of a working simulation or digital twin of the Martian greenhouse demonstrating their AI agent in action.

## **C. Who are the users of this solution?**

* NASA planners  
* Syngenta scientists in other, less extreme settings – for example, designing autonomous cropping systems for use here on Earth

# **4\. Data**

We will provide a knowledge base including the following datasets:

**1\. Mars environmental data**

Gravity, daylight hours, sunlight intensity, atmospheric conditions, soil properties, etc.

**2\. Crop data**

Yield, growth and harvest cycle, nutrition and water requirements, optimum environmental conditions, and response to abiotic stressors

**3\. Human needs**

Nutritional and resource requirements to support a crew of 4 astronauts

Participants are welcome to use other publicly available datasets.

# **5\. Technology**

We will provide an **AWS AgentCore gateway** for the hackers to interact with based on the Syngenta knowledge base.

# **6\. Use Case and Business Case**

Hackers will design an autonomous AI agent system to manage a Martian greenhouse to supplement the diet of a crew of 4 astronauts for a **450-day surface-stay mission on Mars**.

The aim is to:

* Maximize nutrient output  
* Ensure dietary balance  
* Minimize resource consumption
